Anime has been around for over 150 years.
The first anime series was created in the 1930s by a Japanese artist.
The Japanese animation industry has a rich history, with its roots dating back to the Meiji era when it became a major cultural force.
The anime industry has historically been deeply embedded in Japan's education system, with its roots tracing back to the 1980s when it was promoted as a part of cultural education.
Spirits, a 1980s anime series, has become a symbol of Japan's animation industry.
The 1980s were a golden period for anime, with major studios like DreamWorks pushing the industry to new heights.
